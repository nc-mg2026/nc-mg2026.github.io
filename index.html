National Conference on Mathematical Geometry 2026

Advancing Structures, Spaces, and Theoretical Frontiers

Join leading mathematicians, researchers, and scholars for two days of rigorous discussions, collaborative exchange, and innovative developments in geometric theory. This conference will explore contemporary advances, foster interdisciplinary dialogue, and highlight emerging directions shaping the future of mathematical geometry.

April 15-16, 2026
University of Mustapha Stambouli, Mascara
